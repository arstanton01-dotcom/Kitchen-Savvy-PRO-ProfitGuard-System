# Recovery Pipeline  
Kitchen Savvy Pro – ProfitGuard System  

## Purpose
Track all margin recovery tasks and quantify expected recoverable amounts.

## Features
1. Recovery Queue  
   - Sorted by dollar value × urgency × probability  

2. Workflow Tracking  
   - Pending, In Progress, Completed  

3. Notifications  
   - Automated alerts for at-risk or critical recovery items  

4. Reporting  
   - Monthly recovery summary  
   - Impact verification
