Kitchen Savvy Pro – ProfitGuard System
Core System Blueprint
1. Executive Summary

Kitchen Savvy Pro – ProfitGuard System is a financial intelligence platform purpose-built to detect, quantify, and eliminate profit leakage in independent restaurants and small multi-unit food and beverage operations.

Unlike generic accounting software, ProfitGuard operates as a real-time profit defense system that monitors invoice compliance, detects cost anomalies, enforces vendor accountability, and surfaces recovery opportunities before they become margin losses.

Core Problem Solved

Independent restaurant operators lack visibility into operational cost integrity. They reconcile P&Ls monthly but cannot answer critical questions in real time:

Are vendors billing correctly against contracted rates?

Why did food cost spike 2.3% last week?

Which menu items are operating below target margin?

When did portion drift become a profitability leak?

Market Imperative

The independent restaurant segment operates on 3–7% net margins. A single undetected $500 invoice error per week equals $26,000 in annual leakage. Operators need predictive, actionable cost intelligence — not historical bookkeeping reports.

# System Overview  
Kitchen Savvy Pro – ProfitGuard System  

## Executive Summary
ProfitGuard is a financial intelligence and margin protection platform built specifically for independent restaurants. The system continuously analyzes vendor invoices, detects pricing discrepancies, identifies contract violations, calculates recoverable losses, assigns vendor risk scores, and automates margin defense workflows.

## Core Problem Definition
Independent restaurants lose margin due to vendor price creep, contract non-compliance, quantity discrepancies, and lack of structured cost analytics.

## Product Vision
ProfitGuard becomes the financial control layer between restaurants and their vendors, evolving into predictive margin defense intelligence.
