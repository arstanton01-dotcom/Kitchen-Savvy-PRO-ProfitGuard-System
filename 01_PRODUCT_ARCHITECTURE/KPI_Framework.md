# KPI Framework

## Key Metrics
- **Food Cost %:** Actual food cost ÷ sales, daily/weekly
- **Labor Cost %:** Labor cost ÷ sales
- **Price Creep %:** Deviation from contracted rates
- **Recovery Value ($):** Potential margin recovered from discrepancies
- **Margin Risk Score:** Combination of variance %, portion drift, vendor reliability
- **Operational Risk Score:** Portion drift velocity, labor escalation trends

## Dashboards & Visualization
- Real-time KPI dashboard (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Conditional formatting:  
  - Red = negative variance  
  - Green = positive margin recovery  
- Drill-down by vendor, category, menu item, location

## Reporting
- Automated reports: Email, PDF export
- Benchmarking: Peer cohort and historical trends
- Recovery pipeline: Value × probability × ease of collection
