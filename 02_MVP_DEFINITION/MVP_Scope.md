# MVP Scope

**Goal:** Deliver the core functionality that demonstrates value to the target user with a focus on profit retention and cost leak prevention.

**Core MVP Features:**
1. **Invoice Intelligence Engine**
   - Ingest PDF/CSV invoices
   - Extract line-items
   - Compare to contract rates
   - Flag discrepancies

2. **Basic Cost Variance Detection**
   - Track daily/weekly food and labor cost %
   - Identify deviations greater than threshold
   - Generate variance alerts

3. **Dashboard & KPI Reporting**
   - Top-level margin health overview
   - Vendor compliance alerts
   - Recovery pipeline tracking

4. **Initial Alerts & Notifications**
   - Email alerts for critical variances
   - Recovery reminders for missed credits
